OUR DIVISIONS

Each division is defined with a clear, concise tagline.

School of AI

Tagline: Think. Analyze. Predict. Create.

This division helps learners understand modern intelligent systems, covering AI fundamentals, automation logic, prompt engineering, generative tools, machine intelligence, and real-world AI applications. Students learn how AI shapes industries and how to use AI ethically and effectively.

School of Robotics

Tagline: Build. Program. Control. Innovate.

Learners work with sensors, circuits, controllers, mechanical systems, and DIY kits. Using STEM/STEAM methodology and Pictoblox-based projects, students design, build, and program robots that enhance creativity, engineering thinking, teamwork, and problem-solving skills.

School of Finance

Tagline: Earn. Save. Grow. Secure.

This division covers digital banking, budgeting, investment basics, financial planning, economic awareness, and responsible decision-making. Students develop lifelong financial confidence and knowledge.

School of Business

Tagline: Lead. Communicate. Strategize. Succeed.

Students learn communication, leadership, professional etiquette, behavioural skills, entrepreneurship, digital operations, and marketing fundamentals. The School of Business prepares learners to function confidently in real-world professional environments.
