The Harkness Method: Our Core Approach

The Harkness Method places dialogue at the center of learning. Instead of lectures, learners engage in open discussions where every voice matters.
This approach builds:

Critical thinking

Confidence

Active participation

Collaboration

Real-world problem-solving skills

Benefits of the Harkness Method

Encourages engagement and active participation

Allows every student to express ideas

Deepens understanding through dialogue

Improves retention of information

Enhances teamwork and communication

Prepares learners for real-world scenarios
